BSides Luxembourg 2018 CTF

BSides Luxembourg 2018 Capture the Flag is organized and sponsored by the Avatao team.

The CTF starts on 19th October at 09:00 CEST, ends at 17:00 CEST and you can play it here (the URL comes live at that time).

You can already register your user account at Avatao platform and start practicing with other challenges!

Prizes for the winner and runner-ups:

  • CTF winner gets 12 months of premium access to the Avatao platform;
  • Runner-up gets 6 months of premium access to the Avatao platform;
  • Second runner-up gets 3 months of premium access to the Avatao platform.

Registration will stay open for another week after the conference.
